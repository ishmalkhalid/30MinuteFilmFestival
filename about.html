ABOUT US
WHAT IS LOLLYWOOD LTD.?
Lollywood ltd. is a 21st century film studio which produces billion dollar budget films starring actors and actresses from all around the globe. The studio's headquarters are located in the United Arab Emirates and it is also known as the first primarily South Asian film industry located in the Middle East.
WHAT IS 30MFF?
30MFF - also known as the 30 minute film festival - is a project for an Interactive Media course called Communications Lab. Students are required to produce and shoot a film is 30 minutes that is centred around an original and creative storyline.
